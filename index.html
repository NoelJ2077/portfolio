About Me

Introduction to My Journey
 Welcome to my corner of the web! I crafted this custom website during my spare moments while apprenticing at the Bénédict School in Zurich Altstetten. As I type these lines of code, I find myself immersed in my apprenticeship journey as a aspiring software developer.

Passion for Technology & Software
 From a young age, I've been captivated by technology. I vividly remember disassembling electronic devices such as model locomotive remote controls and TV remote controls at the age of 12, just to see how they worked. My earliest gaming memories revolve around titles like Mario Kart and Donkey Kong 64 on my Nintendo DS 64, followed by competitive Minecraft sessions on a square screen, achieving 40fps. While apprenticing in an electronics workshop, I discovered a deeper affinity for IT, driven by YouTube channels like Linus Tech Tips and JayzTwoCents , as well as Fortnite gaming sessions with friends. This passion led me to pursue a career in IT, specifically as a software developer.

Ubuntu Server Knowledge
 Currently, I host my own Monero Node as well as a FluxNode which brings in approximately 15% APY. I also started crypto mining back in early 2021 with Nvidia GPUs. However, when Ethereum switched to Proof of Stake in late 2022 (PoS), profitability significantly decreased, and high electricity prices made it impractical for me to continue mining. As a result, I decided to sell my GPUs and install and invest a FluxNode, which has been a good decision so far. Consequently, I learned a lot about Ubuntu Server (20.04 LTS) and also how to host websites with nginx or a Python script for a Telegram bot.
